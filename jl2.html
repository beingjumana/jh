A LETTER TO THE SWEETEST MOM

Dear Mom,

There’s no bond like a bond between mother and child. You know me in ways no one else ever will.
You understand me so well that we speak and think same.
You also see in me all the things I can still become and continue having faith that I can do and be whatever I want, no matter what my age.
You are my biggest fan, my mentor,my bestie and the first person I call to tell when things are going great or wrong .
The comfort that comes from our friendship,
and your unquestioning support of whatever I do, is irreplaceable.
I wear your unconditional love and support around me like a big hug every single day. I am so blessed to have you in my life. Thank you for always, always being there in every situation.
I hope you break 100 with good health and a strong mind. There’ll never be a day in my life when I won’t need you. I’m here for you too. Please remember that.

Happy, Happy Happiest Birthday Mommie.
You make the world a better place.
Love you always,

Your lovely Daughter

Tap on my lips
